Key Figures

•	Academic mentor and confidant.
•	Provides legitimate cover for her manuscript work.
•	She respects and admires his work.

Heinrich Kaden
•	Warehouse foreman at the Handelskai
•	Pavlova’s quiet informant
•	Provides ledger entries, shipment notes, and rumors
•	She feels responsible for his safety
Countess Irina Volkova
•	Leader of the White Russian occult faction
•	Controls the St. Basil Relief Committee
•	Receives ✠ marked shipments from Istanbul
•	Pavlova fears her influence and ruthlessness
Baron Otto von Hofler & Ilsa von Hofler
•	Leaders of the Viennese occultist faction
•	Seek manuscripts for mystical, not political, reasons
•	Unaware of Soviet or émigré agendas
•	Their thefts complicate Pavlova’s work
Soviet Rezidentura
•	Pavlova’s handlers.
•	Meets her in cafés in the 3rd and 4th districts.
•	Issues vague directives.
•	Conceal the true purpose of the Library hunt. 
Austrian Political Police (Abteilung II)
•	She knows she is under observation.
•	Adjusts routes and cancels meetings when tailed. 
•	Fears they may connect her to smuggled manuscripts.
•	Watching emigres, occultists and anyone near the Handelskai. 
The Adventurers
•	Potential allies
•	Potential liabilities
•	She tests their reliability before trusting them
•	Wants to know who they are working for and the purpose of their interest in the Library.
